Word Hunt Game

How to Play

Your goal is to find all the hidden words in the grid. These words are listed in the "Words to Find" section.

  1. Click and drag on the letters in the grid to select them.
  2. Tap the first letter of the word, and the last letter of it to automatically check the word if you chose the manual selection mode.
  3. You can manually select cells by choosing the tap-to-select on the selection mode.
  4. Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  5. Selected letters must form a straight line â€” no zigzagging.
  6. You can zoom in-and-out to see the whole grid better.
  7. When a correct word is found, it will be highlighted and added to the "Found Words" list.
  8. Each found word also includes a short explanation in the "Word Explanations" section.
  9. Find all words to complete the game!
