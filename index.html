How to Play

Your goal is to find all the hidden words in the grid. These words are listed in the "Words to Find" section.

  1. Click and drag on the letters in the grid to select them.
  2. Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  3. Selected letters must form a straight line â€” no zigzagging.
  4. When a correct word is found, it will be highlighted and added to the "Found Words" list.
  5. Each found word also includes a short explanation in the "Word Explanations" section.
  6. Find all words to complete the game!
